Some of the Cinemas we've partnered with in Kenya include;

1. IMAX - Being one of the largest Cinemas to be built in Kneya, it has served over 100,000 Kenyans and helped them catch up to movies that were in cinemas before they were aired. The IMAX Theatre is located in the XX Century Plaza on Mama Ngina Streets next to City Hall in Nairobi. It was opened in 2012 and ever since functioned as a Family movie theatre and first run movies. It is believed to be the largest ever built cinematic hall in Nairobi.

2. 20th Centuary Cinema - Is one of the oldest built cinemas in Kenya around the 1960s. The 20th Century Cinema is located on Government Road (now re-named Mama Ngina Street). It was built and opened around 1960. At the time it was the most luxurious cinema in the city.

3. Kenya Cinemas - One of the largest cinemas in the coastal region of Kenya. Located in Nkrumah Road in Mombasa, it has served over 50,000 kenyans as well as foreigners who come to tour on the coastal region of Kenya. It has a capacity of 800 seats of which are all fixed facing one extra large screen.
